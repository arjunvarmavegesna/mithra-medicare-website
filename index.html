What We Offer

Our Medical Services

Comprehensive healthcare across a wide range of specialties — all delivered with precision, care, and the latest medical practices.

🔪

General Surgery

Appendectomy, hernia repair, laparoscopic procedures, wound management, and a full range of general surgical interventions.

🤰

Gynaecology & Obstetrics

Antenatal care, safe deliveries, gynaecological surgeries, PCOS management, and complete women's reproductive health services.

🫁

Gastroenterology

Diagnosis and treatment of digestive disorders including liver disease, IBS, acid reflux, colonoscopy, and endoscopy procedures.

✨

Dermatology

Skin disease treatment, acne management, allergy testing, venereology, and cosmetic dermatology consultations.

🦴

Orthopaedics

Bone and joint care, fracture management, joint replacement consultations, sports injury rehabilitation, and spine care.

💊

General Medicine

Internal medicine, diabetes management (CPCDM certified), hypertension, fever, infection treatment, and chronic disease care.

👶

Paediatrics

Complete child healthcare from newborn to adolescent — vaccinations, growth monitoring, fever management, and developmental care.

📡

Radiology & Imaging

X-rays, ultrasound, and diagnostic imaging services with expert radiological interpretation for accurate diagnosis.

👂

ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat)

Treatment of hearing loss, sinusitis, tonsillitis, nasal polyps, throat disorders, and ENT surgical procedures.

🫀

Pulmonology

Asthma, COPD, respiratory infections, sleep apnea evaluation, spirometry, and comprehensive lung health management.

💉

Anaesthesia Services

Expert anaesthesia care for surgical procedures, pain management consultations, and critical care anaesthetic support.

🔬

Gastro Surgery

Advanced gastrointestinal surgical procedures including laparoscopic GI surgery, colorectal surgery, and hepatobiliary interventions.
